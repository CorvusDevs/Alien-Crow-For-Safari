A native **Safari Web Extension** for macOS, iOS, and iPadOS that brings back the classic browsing experience and powers it up. Install it, open a thread, and Alien Crow takes over with Dark Mode and 20+ themes, an ad and annoyance blocker, comment power-ups, keyboard navigation, media tools, and dozens more modules. Everything runs in your browser, with no ads and no tracking.

## Features

- **Dark Mode and 20+ themes.** A polished dark mode plus Dracula, Nord, Catppuccin, Solarized, Tokyo Night, OLED black, and more.
- **Ad and annoyance blocker.** Strip promoted posts, app nags, login walls, chat widgets, cookie banners, and NFT and gold promos.
- **Comment power-ups.** Auto-collapse AutoModerator, mark controversial comments, persistent collapse across visits, color-coded threads, custom depth limits, and jumping between top-level comments.
- **Keyboard navigation and command palette.** Move through posts and comments without the mouse, and put every subreddit, setting, and action one keystroke away.
- **Saved content manager.** A unified viewer for bookmarked posts and saved comments with search, tags, and import/export.
- **View deleted content.** One click reveals removed comments through Reveddit.
- **Live threads.** New comments appear automatically without reloading the page.
- **Inbox preview.** Read messages and replies in a popup without leaving the page.
- **Enhanced search.** Add sort, time, flair, and operator filters, and click any flair to search for it.
- **User insights.** Profile pictures, a history analyzer, persistent user tags, and OP, mod, and admin badges.
- **Layout tweaks.** Wide mode, hidden sidebar, compact posts, zen mode, and multi-column feeds.
- **Media and gallery tools.** Browse image galleries with the arrow keys and view media inline.
- **RSS feeds.** Turn any subreddit, user, or search into a clean RSS feed for your reader.
- **Mobile-friendly old.reddit.** On iPhone and iPad, the classic interface reflows into a touch-friendly layout with proper tap targets.
- **Account switcher.** Keep several accounts on hand and switch between them in a click.
- **Filtering and cleanup.** Hide posts by keyword, domain, or flair, strip tracking parameters, and tidy your history.
- **Bionic reading.** Bold the first part of each word to read long threads faster.
- **90+ optional modules.** Every feature can be enabled, disabled, and tuned from the popup or full settings page.

## What makes Alien Crow special

🎨 **A real theme gallery, not just a toggle.** Dark Mode plus more than 20 hand-picked themes including Dracula, Nord, Catppuccin, Solarized, Tokyo Night, and OLED black. Pick a look once and it follows you across every thread.

🧹 **A quieter Reddit.** The ad and annoyance blocker removes promoted posts, app nags, login walls, chat widgets, cookie banners, and NFT and gold promos, so the page you wanted is the page you get.

💬 **Comment tools built for power users.** Auto-collapse AutoModerator, color-coded threads, persistent collapse across visits, controversial-comment markers, custom depth limits, and quick jumps between top-level comments.

⌨️ **Keyboard-first browsing.** A command palette and full keyboard navigation let you move through posts, comments, subreddits, and settings without reaching for the mouse.

🗂️ **Your saved items, finally organized.** A unified saved-content manager with search, tags, and import/export, plus one-click recovery of deleted comments through Reveddit.

📱 **Old Reddit that works on a phone.** On iPhone and iPad, Alien Crow reflows the dense classic layout into a touch-friendly one with proper tap targets, so old.reddit.com feels right on a small screen.

🛡️ **Built for privacy.** Alien Crow runs entirely in your browser. It does not collect, transmit, or share any data, and every setting is stored locally on your device. No account, no server, no tracking.

## Privacy

Alien Crow runs entirely in your browser. It does not collect, transmit, or share any data, and all settings are stored locally on your device. There is no account and no server. See the [privacy policy](https://corvusdevs.github.io/Alien-Crow-For-Safari/privacy.html) for details.

Alien Crow is an independent project. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Reddit Inc. The "Reddit" name is used descriptively to identify the platform this extension enhances.
